Our Services

Corporate Wellness Consulting & Strategy

We’ll provide tools and resources that support long-term sustainable practice, including organizational audits, customized staff training, strategic planning, and optional ongoing coaching & evaluation. We also offer a personalized EAP Program for small businesses. We’ll work with your business or organization to create inclusive wellness spaces where employees feel valued, nurtured, respected, and recognized.

Coaching Entrepreneurs

We’re well-equipped to provide customized guidance in creating a wellness plan that supports you in building a company, a portfolio, or a brand that yields increased profit. We’ll help you align your goals to a strategic action plan conducive to stabilizing your company and positioning you to scale when you’re ready. We’ll go through every aspect of your business to evaluate where you are and articulate where you want to be.

Mental Health Training Program Development

We’ll create a customized wellness curriculum and accompanying materials that you can implement to train your audience, staff, or those you serve. Our training materials are easily adapted to highlight organizational climate and include a consultation to ensure efficient implementation.

Wellness Event Planning

We will plan your wellness-infused event, paying attention to attendee experience and detail that facilitates transformational experiences. We will show up at your events to teach and model wellness best practices that will support the climate and culture of any group and leave attendees feeling empowered and excited about wellness.
